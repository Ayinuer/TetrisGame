How to play ▸

Welcome! Stack shapes to clear rows and keep the board tidy.

Controls

  • ← / → — Move piece left / right
  • ↑ — Rotate piece
  • ↓ — Soft drop
  • Space / Hard drop — Drop piece fast
  • Hold — Save the current piece for later
  • Start / Pause / Reset — Control the game

Scoring & Tips

  • Clear rows to score points — multi-row clears give bigger bonuses.
  • Use Hold to save a helpful piece for a tricky gap.
  • Watch the Next preview to plan ahead.

Tip: Keep your stack even and avoid tall spires.
